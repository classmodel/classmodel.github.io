CLASS Clips

Here you find clips related to the CLASS model and the book Atmospheric Boundary Layer - Integrating Air Chemistry and Land Interactions. The first two introduction clips introduce the CLASS-model and the book. Ten instructions clips explain, thereafter the switches, variables and parameters in CLASS. Within the seven knowledge clips the theory of CLASS is explained.

Introduction to the Book and Clips

The first clip is a general introduction to the clips, the CLASS-model, the book and the atmospheric boundary layer. The second clip explains how to design an experiment with CLASS using the research cycle.
